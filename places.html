Would you like to go Sundarbans Safari?

Visit two UNESCO World Heritage Sites in Bangladesh- Sundarbans and Bagerhat, including an overnight journey on a traditional ferry to Barishal/Hularhat and experiencing the life and sights at Old Dhaka. Sundarbans is the largest mangrove forest on earth, home of Bengal tigers and many birds and wildlife. Bagerhat is a lost Muslim city from the 15th century full of astonishing mosques. Best tour to experience Bangladesh in the shortest possible time.

On this tour, after visiting Dhaka city the whole day, you'll board a traditional ferry in the evening for an overnight journey to Barishal. The next day you'll visit the numerous mosques of Bagerhat, and the Kodla Moth- a 17th century Hindu temple decorated with beautiful terracotta. After that, you'll spend three full days in Sundarbans and return to Dhaka on a train for your departure the next day. If you have an evening flight, the cost for last night's accommodation will be duducted.
